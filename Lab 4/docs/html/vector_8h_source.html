25 /* Basic info about the datastructures

27  3D and 4D vectors (3D + a homogeneous coordinate) are represented
28  as C arrays of length 3 or 4 and can be passed as parameters efficiently
29  since arrays are passed as a pointer, we prefer to use arrays of double floating point
30  numbers rather than single floaing point numbers to avoid efficiency issues with
31  converting the numbers.
32
